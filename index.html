The Coach's Compass: Day 1

The 5-Minute Team Diagnostic

"Instantly Identify Your Team's Biggest Weakness"

The Reality Check

Most coaches guess what's wrong with their team. This tool will let you know exactly where to start.

In 5 minutes, you'll have complete clarity on your team's biggest weakness and the exact training focus that will transform them fastest.

Rating Scale Guide

Needs Vast Improvement Significant weakness, requires focused attention
Average Typical for age group, some inconsistency
Good Above average with occasional lapses
Excellent for the age group Outstanding performance under pressure
Technical Assessment

Ball Control Under Pressure

Can players receive and turn in tight spaces? Do they panic when pressed? First touch quality under match conditions?

Needs Vast Improvement: Players panic when pressed, heavy first touches, lose ball frequently under minimal pressure
Average: Can handle some pressure but inconsistent, technique breaks down when rushed
Good: Comfortable under pressure most of the time, occasional lapses under intense pressure
Excellent for the age group: Calm under intense pressure, use pressure to create opportunities, exceptional for their age
Assessment:

Passing Accuracy & Decision Making

Are they choosing the right pass? Timing of release? Weight and accuracy of passes?

Needs Vast Improvement: Poor pass selection, frequently gives ball away, can't find teammates consistently
Average: Decent passing but poor decisions under pressure, inconsistent weight and timing
Good: Good passing range and decision making, occasional poor choices under intense pressure
Excellent for the age group: Exceptional vision and execution, consistently finds best option available, outstanding for their age
Assessment:

Finishing Composure

Conversion rate in training vs matches? Decision making in the box? Technique under pressure?

Needs Vast Improvement: Miss easy chances, panic in box, much worse in matches than training
Average: Score some goals but inconsistent, poor decisions when rushed, typical for age group
Good: Clinical most of the time, occasional wayward efforts under pressure
Excellent for the age group: Ice-cold finisher, better under pressure, pick corners consistently, exceptional composure for their age
Assessment:

1v1 Situations

Attacking 1v1s - do they commit defenders? Defensive 1v1s - body position and timing? Confidence in individual battles?

Needs Vast Improvement: Avoid 1v1s, lose most individual battles, poor body position and timing
Average: Win some battles but inconsistent, hesitant in key moments, typical success rate for age
Good: Confident in 1v1s, good success rate, occasional mistakes under pressure
Excellent for the age group: Dominant in individual battles, commit defenders, excellent positioning, outstanding for their age

Tactical Assessment

Possession Retention

Do they understand when to keep vs when to release? Movement off the ball to support possession? Recognition of pressure situations?

Needs Vast Improvement: Constantly lose possession, no movement to help teammates, panic under pressure
Average: Keep ball reasonably well but poor game understanding, inconsistent support, typical for age
Good: Good possession play, understand when to release, mostly good movement and support
Excellent for the age group: Exceptional possession intelligence, create space, always available for passes, outstanding game understanding for their age
Assessment:

Defensive Organisation

Shape maintenance when out of possession? Pressing triggers and coordination? Communication and leadership?

Needs Vast Improvement: No shape, players chase ball, no communication or coordination
Average: Some defensive structure but breaks down under pressure, limited talking, typical for age
Good: Good defensive shape most of the time, decent communication and pressing coordination
Excellent for the age group: Outstanding organisation, coordinated pressing, constant communication, exceptional defensive maturity for their age
Assessment:

Transition Speed

How quickly do they switch from attack to defence? From defence to attack? Recognition of transition moments?

Needs Vast Improvement: Slow to react to possession changes, no urgency in transitions
Average: Eventually adjust but takes time, limited transition awareness, typical reaction speed for age
Good: Quick transitions most of the time, good game awareness and reaction speed
Excellent for the age group: Instant reaction to possession changes, exploit transition moments, exceptional game intelligence for their age
Assessment:
Physical Assessment

Match Fitness

Can they maintain intensity for full game? Recovery between high-intensity moments? Energy levels in final 15 minutes?

Needs Vast Improvement: Tired after 20 minutes, can't maintain intensity, poor recovery between efforts
Average: Decent fitness but drops off in second half, slower recovery, typical stamina for age
Good: Good endurance, maintain standards for most of game, decent recovery
Excellent for the age group: Outstanding fitness, same intensity throughout, quick recovery, exceptional stamina for their age
Assessment:

Strength in Duels

Winning aerial battles? Holding off challenges? Physical presence in key moments?

Needs Vast Improvement: Lose most physical battles, avoid contact, weak in duels
Average: Win some duels but inconsistent, struggle against stronger opponents, typical strength for age
Good: Good physical presence, win most battles, compete well in physical situations
Excellent for the age group: Dominant in physical duels, use strength effectively, intimidating presence, exceptional physicality for their age
Assessment:
Mental Assessment

Confidence Under Pressure

Do they hide when the game gets tough? Vocal leadership and encouragement? Body language when things go wrong?

Needs Vast Improvement: Hide when pressure builds, negative body language, blame others
Average: Some confidence but drops when things go wrong, limited leadership, typical resilience for age
Good: Generally confident, good body language, encourage teammates, handle pressure well
Excellent for the age group: Thrive under pressure, exceptional leadership, lift others when struggling, outstanding mental strength for their age
Assessment:

Concentration & Focus

Maintaining standards throughout session? Listening to instructions? Applying feedback immediately?

Needs Vast Improvement: Easily distracted, don't listen to instructions, repeat same mistakes
Average: Decent focus but lapses, slow to apply feedback, inconsistent attention, typical for age
Good: Good concentration, listen well, usually apply coaching points, maintain focus
Excellent for the age group: Exceptional focus, immediate application of feedback, maintain standards throughout, outstanding concentration for their age
